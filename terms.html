5. Cancellation and Rescheduling Policy

Cancellation by the Student / Parent

We understand that plans change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both parties:

Single Lessons

  • More than 24 hours' notice: The lesson can be rescheduled at no charge, or a full refund will be issued.
  • Less than 24 hours' notice: The lesson will be charged at 50% of the lesson fee. The remaining 50% can be refunded or credited toward a future lesson.
  • No-show (no notice): The full lesson fee will be charged. No refund will be issued.

Lesson Packs (5 or 10 Sessions)

  • Individual sessions within a pack follow the same 24-hour cancellation policy above.
  • Full pack cancellation before any sessions are used: A full refund will be issued.
  • Partial pack cancellation (some sessions used): Remaining unused sessions will be refunded at the single-lesson rate (£25/hr), not the discounted pack rate. For example, if you purchased a 10-pack (£210) and used 4 sessions, the refund would be: £210 − (4 × £25) = £110.

Small Group / Batch Sessions

  • Cancellation policies for group sessions may vary and will be communicated at the time of booking.
  • Generally, group session fees are non-refundable within 48 hours of the session, as your place has been reserved.

Cancellation by the Tutor

If we need to cancel a lesson, we will give as much notice as possible and offer a rescheduled session or full refund. If we cancel a lesson with less than 24 hours' notice, you will receive a complimentary additional 15 minutes added to your next session as a goodwill gesture.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

To cancel or reschedule a lesson, please contact us as early as possible by email at maths@talvika.com or by the messaging method agreed with your tutor. Cancellation notice is counted from the time we receive your message.
