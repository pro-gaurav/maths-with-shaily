Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 May 2026

Who We Are

Maths with Shaily is a private maths tutoring service operated by Shaily Gaurav, based in Woodford Green, London, United Kingdom. Our website is maths.talvika.com. For any privacy-related queries, contact us at maths@talvika.com.

What Data We Collect

When you use our contact form or book a lesson, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Your child's school level and learning needs
  • Session preferences

When you make a payment, payment details (card number, billing address) are processed directly by Stripe. We do not store, see, or have access to your full card details.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and arrange lessons
  • Deliver tutoring sessions and track your child's progress
  • Process payments via Stripe
  • Send lesson reminders and progress updates
  • Communicate about scheduling changes or service updates

We will never use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following lawful bases:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to deliver the tutoring services you have requested and paid for.
  • Legitimate interest: Responding to enquiries submitted via the contact form.
  • Consent: Where you have opted in to receive updates or communications beyond the scope of your booking.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Stripe — for payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy
  • Formspree — for contact form submissions. Formspree's privacy policy: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • GitHub Pages — for website hosting. GitHub's privacy policy: docs.github.com/privacy
  • Zoom / Google Meet — for delivering online lessons.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfil legal obligations. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months. Payment records are retained for 6 years as required by UK tax law (HMRC). If you stop using our services, you may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where no legal obligation to retain it exists)
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, email us at maths@talvika.com.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. We do not use analytics tools such as Google Analytics. No data is collected about your browsing behaviour.

Children's Data

Our service is aimed at school-aged students. Contact and booking information is typically provided by a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
